About

Welcome to the Museums and Art Galleries of Glasgow website! The purpose of this site is to give tourists who are interested in the arts of Glasgow an idea of what we have here for them. On here you can find information on the top five must see artistic venues we have in the city for tourists and locals alike. On the site you will find details on the following museums and art galleries here in Glasgow:

  1. Gallery of Modern Art
  2. The Huntarian Museum
  3. The People's Palace
  4. Kelvingrove Art Gallery
  5. Riverside Museum
